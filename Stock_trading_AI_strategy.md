### Summary of Video Content: Using AI-Powered Software for Options Trading in 2026

This video presents an in-depth overview of a proprietary AI-driven platform, **trackmyoptions.com**, designed to automate and simplify options trading decisions in the stock market. The creator emphasizes that the software is freely accessible—no credit card or promotional code is necessary—and explains how it empowers users to make informed trading decisions efficiently.

### Key Features and Workflow

- **Stock Selection Philosophy**  
  The creator only trades options on stocks he would be willing to own long-term (years). The fundamental criteria for stock selection include:
  - **Growing recent and overall revenue**  
  - **Growing recent and overall cash flow**  
  - **Positive free cash flow (recent and overall)**  
  These financial metrics serve as a filter to identify high-quality stocks suitable for options trading.

- **Benjamin Graham Formula**  
  Used to estimate the **intrinsic value** of stocks and to ensure that trades are made on undervalued or fairly valued securities that meet the above criteria.

- **Automated Stock Grading**  
  The software automatically grades stocks (e.g., "A-grade") based on the financial criteria above, removing the manual effort of checking financial reports on sites like Yahoo Finance. This grading is part of the **stock grader** feature.

- **Genie Mode & Hot List**  
  Genie Mode quickly identifies stocks meeting all criteria, presenting a curated hot list of stocks ideal for options trading. This significantly reduces the time needed to scan hundreds of stocks.

- **Options Contract Screening**  
  Users can type a stock symbol (e.g., IBM, Apple, Nike) into the **cash secured put screener** to instantly view viable options contracts along with metrics such as ROI, strike price, and expiration dates. This replaces manual searches on other platforms.

- **Opportunities Tab**  
  This feature scans the market daily to find the best available options trades filtered by:
  - Stock grade (e.g., A-grade)  
  - Expected ROI  
  - Account size filters (e.g., $2,500 to $10,000)  
  - Distance from strike price (Out-of-The-Money percentage)  
  Example: An Affirm trade providing a 30% in-the-money return shortly after initiation was highlighted.

- **Real-Time Community & Social Features**  
  - Chat box for live interaction among users and the creator.  
  - Automated posting of trade wins shared by community members.  
  - Leaderboards and community impact statistics (e.g., **93% win rate** from the software).  
  - Total premiums collected across the community tracked in real time.

- **Roll Analyzer**  
  AI-powered tool to evaluate open option positions and recommend actions such as holding, closing, or rolling trades based on a customizable profit threshold (commonly 80%).

- **Unusual Volume Scanner**  
  Analyzes the last 365 days of stock volumes to detect unusual spikes, which can signal trading opportunities or market-moving news. This feature helps identify local tops and bottoms by spotting volume outliers.

- **Credit Spread Screener**  
  Designed for smaller accounts, this tool identifies credit spread opportunities with lower capital requirements compared to outright put selling. It highlights trade-offs such as:
  - Maximum loss risk (e.g., losing the collateral in credit spreads if the stock falls below the spread range).  
  - Targeted returns (e.g., 20% annualized ROI on credit spreads, but with caution advised).
